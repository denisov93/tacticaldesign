Car warning on the 9th floor of a company building.
(photo taken last week)

Misleading hazard information

The photo probably looks quite normal, that is... until you realize there is this on the door of a kitchen:

All right, you may still think that there is nothing special about it, however, this is an door to a terrace
... on the ninth floor
... of a serious company
... that being opaque, doesn't let you see what's on the other side.

Although there is no risk, at least involving cars, this misleading warning sign right next to other informational signs may cause confusion for users, especially in emergency situations.

The metal door was probably brought from one of the underground floors without much attention to artifacts.
This warning should be removed or, at most, replaced with another warning about heights and danger of falling.
